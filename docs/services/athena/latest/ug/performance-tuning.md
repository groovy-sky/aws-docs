# Optimize Athena performance

This topic provides general information and specific suggestions for improving the
performance of your Athena queries, and how to work around errors related to limits and
resource usage.

Broadly speaking, optimizations can be grouped into service, query, and data structure
categories. Decisions made at the service level, on how you write your queries, and on how
you structure your data and tables can all influence performance.
