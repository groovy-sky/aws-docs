# Geographic restrictions

If you need to prevent users in selected countries from accessing your content, you can
configure your CloudFront distribution with an **Allowlist** or a
**Block list**. There is no additional charge for
configuring geographic restrictions. For more information, see [Restrict the geographic distribution of your content](https://docs.aws.amazon.com/AmazonCloudFront/latest/DeveloperGuide/georestrictions.html).
