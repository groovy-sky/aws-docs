# Enhancing an Amazon Q Business application environment

After you finish configuring your application environment, you can optionally choose to enhance
it.

You can choose from the following available enhancements:

- **Data sources** – Connect data sources to
your application environment.

- **Admin controls and guardrails** – Customize
blocked topics and choose the knowledge sources your web experience uses for
responses.

- **Response customization** – Configure how
Amazon Q Business generates and formats responses to user queries,
including style, tone, and length.

- **Amazon Q Apps** – Create lightweight,
purpose-built apps within your broader Amazon Q Business application environment. Using enterprise
data, users can create a generative AI-powered app that streamlines their
tasks.

- **Plugins** – Enable your end users to perform
specific tasks related to third-party services from within their web experience
chat—like creating Jira tickets.

- **Document enrichment** – Control document
attribute ingestion and build customized data solutions.

- **Metadata boosting** – Use document
attributes to boost response generation from specific content within your
application environment.

- **Metadata controls** – Enable document
attributes for end user search.
