# TLDs that don't support privacy protection

The following TLDs do not support privacy protection. If your domain uses one of
these TLDs, your contact information will be publicly visible in WHOIS
queries:

Generic TLDs

.audio, .ceo, .diet, .flowers, .guitars, .hosting, .juegos,
.property, .sexy

Geographic TLDs

.cl (Chile), .com.ar (Argentina), .com.au (Australia), .com.br
(Brazil), .com.sg (Singapore), .de (Germany), .es (Spain), .in
(India), .net.au (Australia), .qa (Qatar), .ru (Russia), .sg
(Singapore), .us (United States), .vg (British Virgin
Islands)

###### Note

This list includes the most common TLDs that don't support privacy
protection. For complete and up-to-date information about privacy protection
support for a specific TLD, see the individual TLD page in [Domains that you can register with Amazon Route 53](registrar-tld-list.md).
