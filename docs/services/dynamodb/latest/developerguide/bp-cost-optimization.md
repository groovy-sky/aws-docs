# Optimizing costs on DynamoDB tables

This section covers best practices on how to optimize costs for your existing DynamoDB tables.
You should look at the following strategies to see which cost optimization strategy best suits
your needs and approach them iteratively. Each strategy will provide an overview of what might
be impacting your costs, what signs to look for, and prescriptive guidance on how to reduce
them.
