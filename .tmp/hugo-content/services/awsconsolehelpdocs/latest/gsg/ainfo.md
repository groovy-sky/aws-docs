# Accessing account information in the AWS Management Console

If you have the necessary permissions, you can access information about your AWS account
from the console.

###### To access your account information

1. Sign in to the [AWS Management Console](https://console.aws.amazon.com/).

2. On the navigation bar, choose your account name.

3. Choose **Account**.

4. View your account information.

###### Note

If you would like to close your AWS account, see [Close an AWS account](../../../accounts/latest/reference/manage-acct-closing.md) in the _AWS Account Management Reference Guide_.
