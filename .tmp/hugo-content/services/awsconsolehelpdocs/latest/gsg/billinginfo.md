# Accessing billing information in the AWS Management Console

If you have the necessary permissions, you can access information about your AWS charges
from the console.

###### To access your billing information

1. Sign in to the [AWS Management Console](https://console.aws.amazon.com/).

2. On the navigation bar, choose your account name.

3. Choose **Billing and Cost Management**.

4. Use the AWS Billing and Cost Management dashboard to find a summary and a breakdown of your monthly
    spending.
