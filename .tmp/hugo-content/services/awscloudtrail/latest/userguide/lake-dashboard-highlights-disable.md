# Disable the Highlights dashboard with the CloudTrail console

This section describes how to disable the Highlights dashboard. Because termination
protection is automatically enabled for the Highlights dashboard, you'll need to first
disable termination protection and then disable the Highlights dashboard.

###### To disable the Highlights dashboard

1. Sign in to the AWS Management Console and open the CloudTrail console at
    [https://console.aws.amazon.com/cloudtrail/](https://console.aws.amazon.com/cloudtrail).

2. In the left navigation pane, under **Lake**, choose
    **Dashboard**.

3. Choose the **Highlights** tab.

4. From **Actions**, choose **Change termination**
**protection**.

5. Choose **Disabled**.

6. Choose **Save**.

7. From **Actions**, choose **Disable**
**Highlights**.
