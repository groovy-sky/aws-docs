# Remove a widget from a dashboard with the CloudTrail console

This section describes to remove a widget from a custom dashboard.

###### To remove a widget from a dashboard

1. Sign in to the AWS Management Console and open the CloudTrail console at
    [https://console.aws.amazon.com/cloudtrail/](https://console.aws.amazon.com/cloudtrail).

2. In the left navigation pane, under **Lake**, choose
    **Dashboard**.

3. Choose the **Managed and custom dashboards** tab.

4. In **Custom dashboards**, choose the dashboard for which you want to remove a widget.

5. From **Actions**, choose **Edit**
**dashboard**.

6. On the widget you want to remove, choose the remove icon (![Vertical ellipsis icon representing a menu or more options.](https://docs.aws.amazon.com/images/awscloudtrail/latest/userguide/images/remove-icon.png)) and then choose **Remove**.

7. Choose **Save** to save the dashboard.
