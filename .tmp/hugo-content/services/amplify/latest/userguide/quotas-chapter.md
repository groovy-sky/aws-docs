# Amplify Hosting service quotas

The following are the service quotas for AWS Amplify Hosting. Service quotas (previously
referred to as _limits_) are the maximum number of service resources or
operations for your AWS account.

New AWS accounts have reduced apps and concurrent jobs quotas. AWS raises these quotas
automatically based on your usage. You can also request a quota increase.

The Service Quotas console provides information about the quotas for your account. You can use the
Service Quotas console to view default quotas and [request quota increases](https://console.aws.amazon.com/servicequotas/home?) for adjustable quotas. For more information, see [Requesting\
a quota increase](../../../servicequotas/latest/userguide/request-quota-increase.md) in the _Service Quotas User Guide_.
