# View the list of license included applications on your image builder

###### View the list of license included applications on your image builder

To view the list of license included applications on your image builder, follow
these steps.

1. Open the WorkSpaces Applications console at
    [https://console.aws.amazon.com/appstream2](https://console.aws.amazon.com/appstream2).

2. Choose **Images** in the left navigation pane and the
    **Image builder** tab.

3. Select an image builder, and choose **View details**.

4. Review the list of applications and their statuses under **License**
**included applications**.
