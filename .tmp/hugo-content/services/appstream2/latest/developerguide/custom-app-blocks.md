# Custom App Blocks

Elastic fleet streaming instances utilize applications that are installed on virtual
hard disk (VHD) files stored within an Amazon S3 bucket in your account. App blocks with
custom packaging gives you the flexibility to create your own VHD file, and upload
it to an Amazon S3 bucket within your account.
