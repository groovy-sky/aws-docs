# Supported languages for Amazon Q Business

Amazon Q Business is optimized to respond in English. Amazon Q Business only
indexes English language documents when you [connect a\
Amazon Q Business data source](../business-use-dg/data-sources.md) or [directly upload documents](../business-use-dg/upload-docs.md)
into your application. We recommend indexing only English language content.
