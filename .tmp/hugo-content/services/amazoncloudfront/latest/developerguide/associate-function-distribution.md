# Step 4: Associate the function to your distribution

Once you publish your Connection Function, associate it with your mTLS-enabled
distribution to activate certificate revocation checking.

You can associate the function from the distribution settings page or from the
Connection Function's associated distributions table. Navigate to your distribution
settings, scroll to the **Viewer mutual authentication (mTLS)**
section, select your Connection Function, and save the changes.
